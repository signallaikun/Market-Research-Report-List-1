<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Carbon Capture And Storage (CCS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre-Combustion Carbon Capture</li><li>Oxy-Combustion Carbon Capture</li><li>Post-Combustion Carbon Capture</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carbon Capture and Storage (CCS) involves capturing carbon dioxide emissions from industrial processes or power plants to reduce greenhouse gas output. The market types include pre-combustion carbon capture, which removes CO2 before fuel combustion; oxy-combustion carbon capture, utilizing pure oxygen for combustion to create a concentrated CO2 stream; and post-combustion carbon capture, which extracts CO2 after fossil fuels have burned. Each method offers distinct advantages based on the application and efficiency of capturing emissions for subsequent storage.</p></p>
