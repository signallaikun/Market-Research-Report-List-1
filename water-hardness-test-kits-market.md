<p><p>The water hardness test kits market offers two primary types: test tube kits and test strips. Test tube kits involve adding reagents to a water sample in a test tube and observing color changes to determine hardness levels. They provide precise measurements and are suitable for laboratory use. In contrast, test strips are more user-friendly, allowing quick hardness assessments by dipping a strip into the water. They change color based on mineral concentration, providing instant results, making them ideal for home and field applications.</p></p>
