<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Multi Layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reflective polarizing films are categorized into two market types: single-layer and multi-layer films. Single-layer films consist of a single polarizing layer that filters light to reduce glare and enhance contrast. These films are often used in cost-sensitive applications. In contrast, multi-layer films feature multiple layers of polarizing materials, improving light manipulation and increasing efficiency. Multi-layer films tend to offer superior optical performance and are used in high-end displays, optical devices, and advanced imaging technologies, catering to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
