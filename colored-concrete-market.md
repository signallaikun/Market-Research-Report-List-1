<p><ul><li>Acid-based Staining</li><li>Water-based Staining</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The colored concrete market features two primary types of staining: acid-based and water-based. Acid-based staining involves a chemical reaction with the concrete, producing rich, translucent colors that enhance the surface's natural texture. It is durable and ideal for outdoor applications. In contrast, water-based staining provides a wider range of colors and is more environmentally friendly, offering easier application and cleanup. It typically results in a more uniform appearance, making it suitable for both interior and exterior projects.</p></p>
