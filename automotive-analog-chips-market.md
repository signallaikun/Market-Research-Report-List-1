<p><p>The automotive analog chips market includes two primary types: signal chain chips and power management chips. Signal chain chips manage and process various signals within the vehicle, ensuring accurate sensor data and communication between components. Power management chips regulate energy consumption, optimizing battery life and efficiency by controlling power distribution to various systems. Both types are essential for modern vehicles, supporting enhanced performance, safety, and functionality across advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS) and electric vehicle applications.</p></p>
