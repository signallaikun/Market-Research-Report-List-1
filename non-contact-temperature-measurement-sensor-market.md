<p><p>The non-contact temperature measurement sensor market encompasses various technologies, primarily thermopile infrared (IR) sensors and laser-based sensors. Thermopile IR sensors detect thermal radiation emitted by objects, providing accurate temperature readings without physical contact, making them ideal for diverse applications, including HVAC and medical diagnostics. Laser temperature sensors, on the other hand, use focused laser beams to measure temperature from a distance, ensuring precision and ideal for moving or hard-to-reach objects, commonly used in industrial and automotive sectors.</p></p>
