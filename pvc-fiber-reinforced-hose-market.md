<p><p>The PVC fiber reinforced hose market includes two main types: winding wire hose and crocheted wire hose. Winding wire hoses feature a helical reinforcement of steel or PVC wire, providing high pressure resistance and flexibility, ideal for industrial applications. Crocheted wire hoses utilize a woven wire structure, offering greater durability and flexibility, suitable for various fluid and material transfer tasks. Both types enhance the strength and structural integrity of the hoses, making them essential in construction, agriculture, and manufacturing sectors.</p></p>
