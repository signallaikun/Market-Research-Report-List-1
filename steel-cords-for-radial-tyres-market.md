<p><p>Steel cords for radial tires are categorized based on their tensile strength, which affects performance and durability. Normal Tensile (NT) cords offer basic strength for standard applications. High Tensile (HT) cords provide enhanced strength, ideal for performance tires requiring greater load capacity. Super Tensile (ST) cords further improve tensile strength and flexibility for high-performance demands, while Ultra Tensile (UT) cords deliver maximum strength and durability, suitable for extreme conditions and heavy-duty applications. Each type caters to specific tire performance needs.</p></p>
