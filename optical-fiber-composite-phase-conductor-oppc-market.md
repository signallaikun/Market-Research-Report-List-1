<p><strong>The Optical Fiber Composite Phase Conductor (OPPC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Central Tube Structure OPPC</li><li>Layer Stranding Structure OPPC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Fiber Composite Phase Conductor (OPPC) is a technology that integrates optical fibers with conventional power transmission conductors, enhancing communication while transmitting electricity. The market is categorized into two main types: Central Tube Structure OPPC, where optical fibers are housed in a central tube surrounded by aluminum strands for strength; and Layer Stranding Structure OPPC, which features optical fibers wrapped in layers of aluminum wires. Both designs aim to improve efficiency in electrical grids while providing reliable data transmission.</p></p>
