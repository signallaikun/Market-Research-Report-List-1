<p><p>Refrigerant leak monitors are essential for detecting refrigerant leaks in HVAC systems, ensuring both safety and environmental compliance. The market is primarily divided into two types: halide and electronic monitors. Halide monitors utilize a gas-sensitive element that reacts to the presence of halogenated refrigerants, providing visual or audible alerts. In contrast, electronic leak detectors use advanced sensors and technology to identify leaks with greater sensitivity and specificity, offering enhanced accuracy and often featuring digital displays for easier monitoring.</p></p>
