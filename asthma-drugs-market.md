<p><strong>The Asthma Drugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Quick-relief Medications (Rescue Medications)</li><li>Medications for Allergy-induced Asthma</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The asthma drugs market includes two primary types: quick-relief medications and medications for allergy-induced asthma. Quick-relief medications, also known as rescue medications, provide immediate relief from acute asthma symptoms by relaxing airway muscles and improving airflow. These are typically short-acting bronchodilators, used during asthma attacks. On the other hand, medications for allergy-induced asthma target inflammation and hypersensitivity related to allergens, often comprising corticosteroids or leukotriene modifiers, helping to prevent asthma symptoms triggered by allergens.</p></p>
