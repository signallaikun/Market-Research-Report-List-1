<p><p>The parking meter market includes two main types: single-space parking meters and multi-space parking kiosks. Single-space meters are individual units placed beside each parking spot, allowing drivers to pay for their specific space. In contrast, multi-space kiosks serve multiple parking areas, enabling users to pay for several spaces at once, often utilizing a ticketing system for display in vehicles. Both types aim to enhance parking efficiency, streamline payment processes, and improve revenue collection for municipalities.</p></p>
