<p><p>The Chemiluminescence Immunoassay Analyzer market encompasses various assay types. Immunofluorescence utilizes fluorescent dyes to detect specific proteins. Chemiluminescence employs light emission from reactions for sensitive detection. ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) involves enzyme-labeled antibodies to quantify antigens. The Enzyme Linked Fluorescent System combines enzyme reactions with fluorescence. Multiplexed Assay Systems analyze multiple targets simultaneously, enhancing efficiency. Radioimmunoassay uses radioactively labeled antibodies for quantification, though less common today due to safety concerns. Each type serves specific diagnostic and research needs.</p></p>
