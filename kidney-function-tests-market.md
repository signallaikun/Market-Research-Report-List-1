<p><ul><li>Dilution & Concentration Tests</li><li>Clearance Tests</li><li>Urine Tests</li><li>Imaging Tests</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The kidney function tests market encompasses various types to assess renal health. Dilution and concentration tests evaluate the kidney's ability to manage fluid balance. Clearance tests measure the filtration capacity of the kidneys by assessing substances like creatinine or inulin. Urine tests analyze the composition and concentration of urine, providing insights into kidney function. Imaging tests, including ultrasounds and CT scans, visualize the kidneys' structure and detect abnormalities. Together, these tests provide a comprehensive understanding of renal performance and pathology.</p></p>
