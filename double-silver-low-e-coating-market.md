<p><p>The Double Silver Low-E Coating market features two primary types: Pyrolytic and Sputtered low-E coatings. Pyrolytic low-E coatings are manufactured through a high-temperature process, resulting in a single layer that is durable and cost-effective, ideal for various architectural applications. In contrast, Sputtered low-E coatings are produced using a vacuum deposition process, creating multiple thin silver layers. This method provides superior performance in energy efficiency and solar control, making it suitable for high-performance windows in commercial and residential sectors.</p></p>
