<p><p>Vitrified tiles are a type of ceramic tile that undergoes a process of vitrification, which involves heating clay and other raw materials to extreme temperatures. Vitrified tiles come in various designs, colors, and finishes, making them a popular choice for contemporary flooring and wall solutions in residential and commercial spaces.</p><p>The Vitrified Tiles Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.1% during the forecast period.
