<p><p>The food testing market encompasses various systems to ensure safety and quality. Chromatography systems separate components in food samples, allowing for precise identification and quantification of contaminants such as pesticides or additives. Mass spectrometry systems complement this by analyzing the mass of molecules, providing in-depth insights into food composition and potential toxins. Immunoassay systems utilize antibody-based techniques to detect specific allergens or pathogens, ensuring that food products meet regulatory standards and consumer safety requirements. Together, they enhance food safety testing efficacy.</p></p>
