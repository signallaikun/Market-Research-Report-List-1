<p><p>HVAC temperature sensors are critical for maintaining optimal climate control in buildings. The main types include NTC (Negative Temperature Coefficient) thermistors, which decrease resistance as temperature rises; PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) thermistors, which increase resistance with temperature; RTD (Resistance Temperature Detectors), offering high accuracy using metal resistance changes; and thermocouples (TCs), which generate voltage based on temperature differences. Each type has its advantages, catering to various monitoring and control needs within building systems, ensuring efficient energy use and comfort.</p></p>
