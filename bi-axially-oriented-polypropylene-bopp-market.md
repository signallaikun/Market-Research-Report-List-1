<p><strong>The Bi-axially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat film strength method</li><li>Tubular quench method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bi-axially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) is a versatile polymer film known for its excellent strength, clarity, and barrier properties. The BOPP market comprises two main types: the flat film strength method and the tubular quench method. The flat film strength method involves extruding polypropylene into flat sheets, offering uniform thickness and high tensile strength. In contrast, the tubular quench method uses a tubular configuration, allowing for continuous processing and efficient cooling, resulting in higher production output. Both methods cater to diverse packaging and labeling applications.</p></p>
