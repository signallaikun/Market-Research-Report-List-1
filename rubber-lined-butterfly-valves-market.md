<p><p>The rubber-lined butterfly valves market features two main types: wafer type and lugged type. Wafer type valves are compact, flanged between pipe flanges without additional bolting, ideal for space-saving applications. In contrast, lugged type valves have threaded lugs on the body, allowing for easy installation and removal without needing to disconnect the pipeline. Both types ensure efficient flow control and sealing, making them suitable for various industries, including water treatment, chemical processing, and HVAC systems.</p></p>
