<p><p>The robotic exoskeleton market is categorized into three types: lower body, upper body, and full body. Lower body exoskeletons assist individuals with mobility impairments by supporting walking and standing. Upper body exoskeletons aid in enhancing the strength and endurance of the arms, often used in industrial settings for lifting tasks. Full body exoskeletons integrate support for both upper and lower limbs, providing comprehensive assistance for rehabilitation and enhancing physical capabilities in various applications, from healthcare to defense.</p></p>
