<p><p>The rigid-flex PCB market comprises three primary types: single layer, double layer, and multi-layer. Single layer rigid-flex PCBs feature one conductive layer, offering simplicity and lower costs. Double layer PCBs incorporate two conductive layers, enhancing complexity and performance for more demanding applications. Multi-layer rigid-flex PCBs, with three or more layers, provide advanced circuitry capabilities, enabling intricate designs and higher functionality. Each type caters to specific industry needs, balancing cost, performance, and space efficiency in electronic applications.</p></p>
