<p><p>The aerospace power converters market primarily encompasses AC/DC power converters and DC/DC power converters. AC/DC power converters transform alternating current (AC) from sources like generators into direct current (DC) to power onboard systems. These are essential for various avionics and electrical systems. In contrast, DC/DC power converters manage and regulate voltage levels within the aircraft, allowing for efficient power distribution among subsystems. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring reliable and efficient electrical power management in aerospace applications.</p></p>
