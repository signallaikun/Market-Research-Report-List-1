<p><p>The protein chromatography resin market comprises various types, including Protein A, Protein G, and Protein L resins. Protein A resin is primarily used for purifying antibodies due to its high affinity for the Fc region. Protein G resin also targets antibodies, offering a broader range of binding interactions. Protein L resin specifically binds to the kappa light chains of antibodies, making it useful for certain types of immunoglobulins. Each type enhances the efficiency and specificity of protein purification processes in biotechnology and research applications.</p></p>
