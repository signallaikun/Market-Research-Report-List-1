<p><p>The Paint Circulation System (PCS) facilitates the efficient movement of paint in industrial applications. Key market types include the two-pipe system, which uses separate pipes for supply and return, minimizing contamination; the dead end system, where paint circulates without an adjustable return path, risking stagnation; and the third line system, featuring an additional pipe for improved control and cleaning. Other market types may incorporate advanced technologies, enhancing process efficiency and reducing waste in paint application processes.</p></p>
