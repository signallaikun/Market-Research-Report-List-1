<p><p>The PTA (Percutaneous Transluminal Angioplasty) Dilatation Catheter is a medical device used in minimally invasive procedures to treat narrowed or blocked blood vessels. This procedure is particularly beneficial in managing conditions like peripheral artery disease (PAD) and coronary artery disease (CAD).</p><p>The PTA Dilatation Catheter Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by an increase in cardiovascular diseases, advancements in catheter technology, and a growing elderly population.
