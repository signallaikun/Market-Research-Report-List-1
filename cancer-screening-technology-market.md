<p><p>The cancer screening technology market encompasses two primary types: instrument-based inspection and platform-based inspection. Instrument-based inspection involves the use of specialized medical devices, such as imaging systems and biopsies, to detect cancerous cells or lesions directly. In contrast, platform-based inspection utilizes integrated systems that combine various diagnostic tools, often leveraging advanced technologies like AI and genomics to analyze biological samples. Both approaches aim to improve early detection and diagnosis, enhancing patient outcomes and treatment efficacy.</p></p>
