<p><p>The invisible braces market comprises three main types: clear aligners, ceramic braces, and lingual braces. Clear aligners are transparent plastic trays that gradually shift teeth and are removable for convenience. Ceramic braces use tooth-colored brackets to offer a discreet appearance while functioning similarly to traditional braces. Lingual braces are affixed to the back of the teeth, making them virtually invisible from the front. These options cater to varying patient preferences for aesthetics and comfort in orthodontic treatment.</p></p>
