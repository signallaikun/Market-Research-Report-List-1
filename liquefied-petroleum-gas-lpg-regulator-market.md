<p><ul><li>Direct Acting Type</li><li>Spring-Loaded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Liquefied Petroleum Gas (LPG) regulator market comprises two primary types: direct acting and spring-loaded regulators. Direct acting regulators use a simple diaphragm mechanism to regulate gas pressure directly, offering quick response times and ease of use, making them ideal for low-pressure applications. In contrast, spring-loaded regulators utilize a spring mechanism to maintain consistent pressure, providing stability for high-demand systems. Both types are essential for safe and efficient gas distribution in residential, commercial, and industrial applications.</p></p>
