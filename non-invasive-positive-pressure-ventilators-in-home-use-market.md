<p><p>The Non-invasive Positive Pressure Ventilators (NPPV) market for in-home use comprises two primary types: Single Level Ventilators and Bilevel Ventilators. Single Level Ventilators provide a continuous and consistent airflow at one pressure setting, ideal for patients with stable respiratory conditions. In contrast, Bilevel Ventilators offer two distinct pressure settings—one for inhalation and a lower one for exhalation—allowing for more tailored respiratory support, beneficial for patients with variable breathing patterns or specific conditions like obstructive sleep apnea.</p></p>
