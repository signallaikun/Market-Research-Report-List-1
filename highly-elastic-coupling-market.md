<p><ul><li>Spiral Groove Type</li><li>Parallel Groove Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The highly-elastic coupling market primarily includes two types: spiral groove and parallel groove couplings. Spiral groove couplings utilize a helical design to provide flexibility and compensate for misalignments, making them suitable for high-torque applications. In contrast, parallel groove couplings feature parallel grooves that allow for significant angular misalignment while maintaining efficiency. Both types are essential in various industrial sectors, including automotive and manufacturing, due to their ability to enhance performance, reduce vibration, and prolong equipment lifespan.</p></p>
