<p><p>The Automotive Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) System Market is categorized mainly into two types: Pneumatic EGR Valves and Electric EGR Valves. Pneumatic EGR Valves use a diaphragm and vacuum control to regulate the flow of exhaust gases back into the intake, relying on engine pressure. In contrast, Electric EGR Valves utilize electronic actuators for more precise control and responsiveness, improving fuel efficiency and emissions reduction. Both types play a critical role in enhancing engine performance and meeting environmental standards.</p></p>
