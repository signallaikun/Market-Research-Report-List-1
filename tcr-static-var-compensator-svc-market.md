<p><p>The TCR Static Var Compensator (SVC) is an advanced power electronic device used to regulate voltage levels and improve the quality of power in electrical systems. It employs Thyristor-controlled reactors (TCR) to provide dynamic reactive power compensation, enhancing the stability and reliability of electrical networks. SVCs are primarily utilized in industries where power quality is critical, such as utilities, manufacturing, and renewable energy sectors.</p><p>The TCR Static Var Compensator (SVC) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.2% during the forecast period, driven by several factors.
