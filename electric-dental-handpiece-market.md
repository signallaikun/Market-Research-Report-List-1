<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electric Dental Handpiece Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brush Motor</li><li>Brushless Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric dental handpiece market comprises two main types: brush motor and brushless motor handpieces. Brush motors utilize carbon brushes for operation, providing cost-effectiveness but generating more noise and heat. In contrast, brushless motors offer enhanced efficiency, reduced maintenance, and longer lifespan due to the absence of brushes. This leads to quieter operation and smoother performance. Both types cater to varying needs in dental practices, with brushless motors increasingly preferred for their advanced technology and reliability.</p></p>
