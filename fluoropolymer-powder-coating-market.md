<p><p>The fluoropolymer powder coating market is divided into two main types: thermoplastic and thermosetting powder coatings. Thermoplastic powder coatings can be re-melted and reprocessed upon heating, offering flexibility and ease of application. They provide excellent chemical resistance and durability. In contrast, thermosetting powder coatings undergo a chemical reaction during curing, resulting in a hard, permanent finish that is highly resistant to heat and chemicals. Both types are utilized for protective and decorative finishes in various industries.</p></p>
