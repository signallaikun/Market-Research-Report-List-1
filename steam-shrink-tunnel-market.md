<p><ul><li>Single Zone Tunnels</li><li>Dual Zone Tunnels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steam shrink tunnel market is primarily segmented into single zone and dual zone tunnels. Single zone tunnels utilize a single steam chamber for packaging shrink applications, providing a cost-effective solution for small to medium production needs. In contrast, dual zone tunnels feature separate chambers for pre-heating and shrinking, allowing for greater control over temperature and steam conditions. This results in enhanced efficiency and adaptability for high-volume packaging operations. Both types cater to different production scales and operational requirements.</p></p>
