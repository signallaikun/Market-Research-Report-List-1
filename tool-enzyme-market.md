<p><p>The enzyme market encompasses various tools essential for molecular biology, including DNA restriction endonucleases, ligases, polymerases, nucleases, and modification enzymes. Restriction endonucleases cut DNA at specific sequences, while ligases join DNA fragments together. Polymerases are vital for amplifying DNA, aiding in techniques like PCR. Nucleases degrade nucleic acids and are used in various applications, including cloning and sequencing. Modification enzymes, including methyltransferases, add functional groups to DNA, influencing gene expression and regulation. Together, these enzymes enable advanced genetic manipulation and research.</p></p>
