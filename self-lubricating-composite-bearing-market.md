<p><strong>The Self-Lubricating Composite Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metal Matrix Based Composite Bearings</li><li>Fiber Matrix Based Composite Bearings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-lubricating composite bearing market comprises two primary types: metal matrix-based composite bearings and fiber matrix-based composite bearings. Metal matrix composites utilize a metallic base combined with reinforcing materials to enhance strength and wear resistance, making them suitable for high-load applications. In contrast, fiber matrix composites incorporate fibers such as carbon or glass within a polymer or metallic matrix, offering superior wear performance and reduced friction in lighter-weight applications. Both types provide effective lubrication without the need for external oils, enhancing durability and efficiency.</p></p>
