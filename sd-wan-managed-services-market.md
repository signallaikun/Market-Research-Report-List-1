<p><p>The SD-WAN managed services market encompasses several key components: </p><p>1. **SD-WAN Edge**: This involves the devices at branch locations that manage connectivity and optimize traffic.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **SD-WAN Controller**: Centralized management system that governs the network, ensuring policies are enforced across the SD-WAN.</p><p>3. **Service Orchestrator**: Facilitates the integration and coordination of various services within the SD-WAN infrastructure.</p><p>4. **SD-WAN Gateway**: Connects the SD-WAN to external networks or the internet, managing inbound and outbound traffic.</p><p>5. **Subscriber Web Portal**: A user-friendly interface for clients to monitor network performance and manage configurations.</p></p>
