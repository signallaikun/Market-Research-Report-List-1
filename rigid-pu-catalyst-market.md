<p><p>The Rigid PU Catalyst market encompasses two main types: Non-reactive Amine Catalysts and Reactive Amine Catalysts. Non-reactive Amine Catalysts are used primarily to facilitate the polymerization process without becoming part of the final product, ensuring consistent performance in rigid polyurethane formulations. In contrast, Reactive Amine Catalysts actively participate in the chemical reaction, contributing to the final product's characteristics and properties. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing the efficiency and quality of rigid polyurethane foams and materials.</p></p>
