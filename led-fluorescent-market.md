<p><p>The LED fluorescent market features various types, including Direct Contact technology, where LEDs directly excite phosphors for enhanced efficiency. Remote Phosphor involves separating phosphors from the LED source, allowing for better thermal management and color performance. Thin Film technology utilizes a slim layer of phosphor, optimizing light output. Modular Light Engine offers customizable solutions for diverse applications, while Package Free systems eliminate traditional packaging, promoting sustainability and design flexibility. Each type enhances performance and application versatility in the lighting industry.</p></p>
