<p><ul><li>Porous PTFE Glass Fabric</li><li>Non-Porous PTFE Glass Fabric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PTFE fabric market is divided into two main types: porous PTFE glass fabric and non-porous PTFE glass fabric. Porous PTFE glass fabric is characterized by its ability to allow airflow and vapor transmission, making it ideal for applications requiring breathability, such as in filters and protective clothing. In contrast, non-porous PTFE glass fabric provides a watertight barrier, offering superior resistance to liquids and chemicals, which is beneficial for applications in industrial and automotive sectors.</p></p>
